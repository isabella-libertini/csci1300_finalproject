# CSCI 1300 Final Project - Project Description 

## Theme 

The player will run a small diner (Luke's) in Stars Hollow, inspired by the Gilmore Girls universe/world. A competing coffee shop moves in next door. Founder's Day festival can be the final community center bundle and the main goal worked towards.

## Main objective 

Getting everything ready for the Founder's Day festival before you run out of time! (days)
Players will collect festival items (food, decorations, prizes, etc.), build relationships with townspeople, and make choices about managing money and time throughout the game.

## Characters 

All characters the player will be able to interact with and build a relationship with
- 'Luke' = owns Luke's Diner, gives tips or small jobs to do 
- 'Lorelai' = manager of Independence Inn, will give you festival decor if you help her around inn
- 'Ms. Patti' = town gossip, knows everything around town, shows player opportunites around town or warn about rival coffee shop
- 'Mr. Waterhouse' = owns/manages rival coffee shop, offers short cuts with a catch

every interaction and/or favor completed for characters will raise player's friendship with them, which can come with benefits 

## Locations 

Players will be able to walk to 
- Luke's Diner
- The Independence Inn 
- Town Square (where Founder's Festival will be)
- Doose's Market (general store to buy things with enough money)

## Planned classes 

- 'Player' = keeps tracks of player's money, energy, current location, inventory, friendship level with characters, etc.
- 'Character' = this will represent a Stars Hollows resident (name, friendship level, favors needed from player, rewards given back, etc.)
- 'Location = represents a place on the map (name, lsit of characters/items to be found there, etc.)
- 'Item' = something a player can pick up (name, list of characters or items found there)
- 'FestivalGoal' = someway to track which items have been collected 

## Inventory 

The player will have an inventory of things they've pick up (festival supplies, items from Doose's market, rewards from characters, etc.) Items can go towards festival goal

## Limired resources 
- Time = game runs for seven days
- Money = players need money to buy festival items, can earn money working at Luke's Diner

## Plan for handling time

Each day the player can get a set number of actions (3?) like visiting a place, talking to a character, working, etc. 
After using all actions for the day, the day will end and next start 
When time is up, game ends and checks if players finsihed festival prep or not

## Tradeoff system 

Mr. Waterhouse will offer players quick and easy ways to get festival supplies, but lowers friendship with townspeople and costs money instead of time
Player has to decide to get supplies quicker or go to work at Luke's Diner

## Mapping Style 

Print a menu in the terminal that has a list of the four locations with the player's current spot marked

## Win/lose condition 

Win = player collects all festical items and friendship levels aren't horrible by end of the day, Founder's Day is great and Luke's Diner is saved 

Lose = time runs out before play collected all needed items for the festival, it gets cancelled and Starbucks takes over
